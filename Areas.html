Terrain & Weather

Terrain

Each area should have a base terrain or two from among the possibles that correlates with the character of the area & the associated castles. e.g. Tatalia is mostly Swamp in the lowlands, but a bit of rough in the high hills.

In TBS, fighting on native terrain gives +1atk/def/spd like in H3C, but does not reveal landmines or quicksand. Also, having all creatures native to the terrain negates any movement penalty if there is one, and grants bonus movespeed if there isn't.

Types of Terrain

  • Grassland - 0% move penalty
  • Forest - 10% penalty
  • Dirt - 0% penalty
  • Rough - 25% penalty
  • Savannah - 10% penalty
  • Sand - 50% penalty
  • Swamp - 50% penalty
  • Lava - 25% penalty
  • Subterranean - 10% penalty

Weather

Weather consists of rain, snow, wind & extreme heat, each is seasonal, locked in to certain months/days of a year, and varies per area of the map you're on.
