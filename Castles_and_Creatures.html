Castle Design & Creature Factionaliy

Castle General

  • Hall will scale as Village Hall - Town Hall - City Hall - Metropolis Center. Capitol will be special upgrade for known, named places (e.g. Steadwick).
  • Town Hall will visually add fountain (necessary for town portal). City Hall will grow bigger, Metropolis should be biggest & multi-level on all types. Capitol should either be a custom image or more ornate version of Metropolis in same style (e.g. for Castle, lots of ornate, gilded accents. for Inferno, more glowy brimstone) .
  • Town requires only Tavern still, City should require basic Mage Guild, Blacksmith (which will be a single combined building representing arms & armor sales/repair/etc., to be cohesive with MM), and Marketplace (which will be a set of shops representing alchemist, magician & bank, to be cohesive with MM). Metropolis requires Castle still.
  • Upgrading to Metropolis should upgrade the troops garrisoned in the towers. In towns with 2+ troops (Castle, Tower, Stronghold, Conflux, Necropolis & Dungeon), just replace the lowest shooter with the highest one (e.g. Orcs -> Cyclopses) & double the damage. In Rampart, Elves -> Grand Elves and consequently shoot twice. In Fortress, 2 more wall garrisons (for a total of 4) + a 2nd Lizard Archer in the keep for double damage. In Inferno, Gogs -> Magogs improving damage to 1.5x & giving 1 hex fireball ring.
  • Technical: allow for disabling certain structures while enabling the rest of the tech tree to be built. Essentially, allow e.g. a Mage Guild to be bypassed for a magic-less scenario (think Dragon Slayer map 4) but still allow for a City Hall to be built.

Mage Guild

  • Levels are now Basic - Advanced - Expert - Master (no 5th) instead of 1-5
  • Visually, should be a set of 3 buildings (elemental/self/lifedeath), each of which "grow" per level, and a well in the middle (for spell point restoration).
  • Each castle should have an "affiliation" with a certain type of magic that's cohesive with the heroes & the overall theme. Maybe e.g. Castle has a bonus to body & spirit, like those guilds have 1 level higher spells than the actual built guild level or something.

Castle Defense Structures

  • Fort adds basic walls with 1hp each
  • Citadel adds keep (2hp) & gives drawbridge 2hp
  • Castle adds outer towers (2hp) & makes all walls 2hp + keep 3hp
  • Fortification (common bldg, H2C import) makes all walls & towers 3hp. Makes Citadel Keep 3hp (does not make Castle Keep 4hp)
