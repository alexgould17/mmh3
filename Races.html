Sentient Races & Their Playability

The division between sentience & non-sentience, as well as the distinction of who can be played a TBS "hero", RPG character, etc., vs. who is an NPC to be ordered around, is a choice laden with bias. Make all decisions, including language, consciously & critically.

Classes/Jobs

Five categories: All-Might, Might-focused, Balanced, Magic-focused, All-Magic. First is chosen specialization: depends on region, skills, stats. Beyond that are honorific titles akin to 2nd promotions in MM7C. Unlike MM7C, skill masteries should only be gated behind achieving 1st specialization, 2nd should be specialty focus, stat growth & honor. In RPG, chars pick 1 of 5 basic paths to start in, then progress to a specialty by skill choice & accomplishing a quest. Choosing 1 path locks out all others for actual promotion, but quest xp can be gained & "honorary" titles as well like MMC. In TBS, all heroes start at first specialties & can progress to 2nd via experience.

All-Might:

  • Fighter (Basic)
  • RPG Reqs: 25 Mgt/End, 20 Spd/Acc
  • TBS Reqs: 2 Battle skills, 1 Battle/Other
  • Knight - Champion or Paladin
  • RPG Reqs: Knight - 50 Mgt/End, 40 Spd/Acc, Exp in a melee weapon, Adv in Chain or Plate, Bas Smithing; Champion - 100 Mgt/End, 75 Spd/Acc, Mas in melee weap, Exp in Chain or Plate, Exp Smithing; Paladin - 80 Mgt/End, 70 Spd/Acc, 40 Per, Mas in melee weap, Exp in Chain or Plate, Adv Smithing, Bas all 3 self magics
  • TBS Reqs: Knight - 2Atk/2Def, Bas Leadership; Champion - 8Atk/8Def, Exp Leadership + 2 Adv+ Battle skills; Paladin - 6Atk/7Def/1Pwr/2Kno, Exp leadership, 1 Adv+ Battle skill, Bas all 3 self magics
  • Barbarian - Tribal Chieftan or Warlord
  • RPG Reqs: Barbarian - 50 Mgt/Spd, 40 End/Acc, Exp in a melee weapon, Adv any armor skill, Bas Body Building; Warlord - 100 Mgt/Spd, 75 End/Acc, Mas melee weapon, Exp any armor, Exp Body Building or Adv Body Building/Adv Regeneration; Tribal Chieftan - 80 Mgt/Spd, 70 End/Acc, 40Per, Exp melee weap, Adv armor, Adv Body Building or Regen, Adv Spirit & Body Magics
  • TBS Reqs: Barbarian - 3Atk/1Def, Bas Offense; Warlord - 10Atk/6Def, Exp Offense + 2 Adv+ Battle skills; Tribal Chieftan - 6Atk/6Def/2Pwr/2Kno, Adv Offense + 1 more Adv Battle or Other skill, Adv Spirit & Body Magics
  • Rogue - Spy or Assassin
  • RPG Reqs: Rogue - 40 Mgt/End/Spd/Acc/Lck, Adv Dagger/Disarm Trap/Leather; Spy - 80 Lck, 70 Mgt/End/Spd/Acc, 40 Int, Mas Dagger, Exp Disarm Trap/Leather, Adv Air Magic; Assassin - 80 Spd/Acc/Lck, 70 Mgt/End, Mas Dagger, Exp Disarm Trap/Leather, Adv Dodging
  • TBS Reqs: Rogue - 2Atk/1Def (4tot atk/def), 1 Battle skill, 1 Other skill; Spy - 8Atk/5Def/1Pwr/2Kno, 3 Adv skills: at least 1 Battle & at least 1 Other; Assassin - 9Atk/7Def, 3 Adv skills: 2 Battle, 1 Other
  • Beastkeeper - Land Defender
  • RPG Reqs: Beastkeeper - 50 End/Acc, 40 Mgt/Spd, Exp any armor skill, Adv any melee weapon or Bow, Bas ID Monster; Land Defender - 100 End/Acc, 75 Mgt/Spd, Mas any armor, Exp any melee weapon or Bow, Exp ID Monster
  • TBS Reqs: Beastkeeper - 1Atk/3Def, Bas Armorer + 1 Battle skill + 1 Battle/Other skill; Land Defender - 6Atk/10Def, Exp Armorer, 1 Adv+ Battle skill, 1 Adv+ Battle or Other skill
